How old do I have to be allowed to stay overnight at the campsite?

The campsite is intended for people aged 16 years or older.

Cheap and easy with the train?

Each e-festival ticket entitles you to one free e- train ticket that from 08-06-2016 to www.nmbs.be/rockwerchter can be ordered with the SNCB code you can download from 08-06-2016 through www.ticketmaster .net / RW .

The e-train tickets are valid for one outward and one return journey by train in 2nd class from the Belgian station chosen to LEUVEN in the period from 29-06-2016 Wednesday from 11am t.e.m. Monday 04-07-2016 . The e- train ticket is also valid as a ticket in the special buses of De Lijn between Leuven station and the Werchter festival park in the period from 30-06-2016 Thursday from 9am t.e.m. Monday 04-07-2016 at 13h . Only to holders of a camping ticket The Hive is the e- train ticket valid for Line from Wednesday 29-06-2016 from 13h.

Cheap and easy with the bus?

Living a bit too far to go by bike? No problemo, we got several bus stops on a 5 minute walk.

Best bus stops: Sint Andries Kerk, Sint Andries De Prange, Sint Andries Zandstraat & Sint Andries Koude Keuken.

  • Bus 5 (Sint Andries Kerk)
  • Bus 15 (Sint Andries Kerk & Sint Andries De Prange)
  • Bus 55 (Sint Andries Kerk)
  • Bus 89 (Sint Andries Zandstraat & Koude Keuken)
  • Bus 52 & 53 (Sint-Andries Kerk)
