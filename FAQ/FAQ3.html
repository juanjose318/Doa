How old do I have to be allowed to stay overnight at the campsite?

The site is intended for people aged 16 years or older.

Can I eat or drink on the campsite?

DOA Festival has a wide culinary oer at the festival site . At the campsite there are however not eat - or drink - positions. 20 food and beverage voucher , you pay € 40 in advance. The presale runs through our site and runs until Sunday 31st July. At the festival , you pay € 12 for 5 food and beverage coupons.

How much does camping cost during DOA Festival?

The price of your stay at one of the two campsites is included in your DOA Festival 2016 ticket. A day ticket costs € 50 all in ( public transport NMBS and De Lijn, 2 days camping at Camping Relax , including service costs € 4 and € 1 mobility contribution ) . A combi ticket costs € 90 all in ( public transport NMBS and De Lijn, 4 days camping including service costs € 4 and € 1 mobility contribution ).
