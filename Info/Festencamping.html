Festival

DOA Festival 2016 will take place on 24 and 25 September on the familiar Festivalterrein the Cold Kitchen in Bruges. The festival will take place this year for the 1st time . DOA Festival is made possible thanks to the willing cooperation of the city of Bruges and its inhabitants. The information provides an additional service to visitors of DOA Festival. During the festival you will be held here at the height of the program, weather forecasts , information on where everything is located , information about the services and information about public transport. If you have lost something if you've found something that someone else lost , then bring it to the information desk. Visitors can during opening hours, with questions at the information desk , problems, complaints and compliments and one can come up tips and suggestions.
